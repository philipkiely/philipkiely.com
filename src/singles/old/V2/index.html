Scripts

  • TriMap

    Created during the spring 2017 HackISU, TriMap is an online Dungeons and Dragons map creator in an isometric perspective. Add tiles, doors, and levels in an elegent 3D perspective; then place your characters on the map and explore. Use TriMap to keep track of players, NPCs, and other objects to free your mind to focus on what's important: the adventure.

  • Rhyming Line Generator

    This program, when given one of over 1,000 unique words, returns a line of iambic pentameter that rhymes with the given word or creates a full sonnet. The program uses all 152 of Shakespeare's complete sonnets to generate these lines, as sonnets have a strict meter and rhyme scheme.

  • Spectrum Compose

    Finding typing boring? This online text editor changes background color with every keystroke to make typing even the most boring report exciting. Note that the flashing is very distracting and there is no way to format or save the text, making this pretty useless, but it's easier to quit than vim!
